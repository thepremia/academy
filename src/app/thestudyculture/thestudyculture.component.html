Our Curriculum

An integrated curriculum extends a framework with significant and expressive learning including assessments and evaluation to meet the standards and benchmarks of the boards whose learning objectives are the guide. Learning through integration stimulates the development of creativity, critical thinking and problem-solving. Integration also develops Communication and collaboration within students. Also, digital literacy, social and cultural skills, prove to be beneficial, providing students with opportunities to acquire enduring skills for life. The attributes of an integrated curriculum include experiences to develop children’s attitudes, skills, and knowledge exposing them to a range of activities to develop and sustain in the ever-changing and challenging world. These advantages lead to better opportunities for teacher-student planning and cooperative evaluation which leads to enhanced creative teaching and learning activities. The process of learning and teaching from a fusion of knowledge with various activities, resources; and experiencing real-life situations for a solution-based approach. It also instils problem-solving, critical thinking and collaboration in and beyond the classroom. The Premia curriculum will be student-centric with flexibility, innovation and standards of learning outcomes for academic achievements as well as core values essential for practical life experiences.
